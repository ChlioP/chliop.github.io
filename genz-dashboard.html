Gen Z Travel Behavior Dashboard

Built with Tableau, Google Sheets, and Visual Storytelling

Dashboard Preview

This project analyzes mock data representing travel behavior among Gen Z tourists. It explores their preferences for destinations, travel seasons, booking methods, spending habits, and content-sharing platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

Tools & Technologies

  • Google Sheets (data design & cleaning)
  • Tableau Public (for dashboard visuals)
  • Figma (UI reference design)
  • Python + Faker (optional mock data generation)

Dashboard Highlights

  • Top destinations and most common travel months
  • Preferred booking platforms (TikTok, Klook, Traveloka)
  • Average travel budget by country
  • Social media trends: Where Gen Z shares their trips
  • Reasons for travel: Food, aesthetics, culture, relaxation

Key Insights

  • TikTok is the most influential platform for travel planning
  • Gen Z prefers Southeast Asian destinations with aesthetic value and affordable cost
  • Travel reasons are emotionally driven: food experience, photography spots, nightlife
  • Peak travel occurs in March, July, and September
